About S.H.E

S.H.E which stands for the Safe Home for Everyone is a project created to help victims of Gender Based Violence in South Africa. We are a service that is all about inclusivity hence the name S.H.E which inclides H.E as well. This is just to show we are all about inclusivity and believe gender based violence does not target a specific gender. S.H.E consists of 3 parts namly, Emergency assist, Safe Home and, Rebuild
